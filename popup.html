ℹ️ Using Different Google Accounts

This extension analyzes the Gmail inbox of your current Chrome profile's Google account.

To analyze a different Gmail account:
1. Click your Chrome profile icon (top-right corner of browser)
2. Select "Add" to create a new profile
3. Sign in with the Google account you want to analyze
4. Load this extension in that profile
5. Click "Analyze Inbox"

Quick switch: Use the profile switcher (top-right) to toggle between your profiles.
